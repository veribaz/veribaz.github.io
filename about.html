Our Story

We started Veribaz in 2019 because we were tired of using software that didn't respect our time. Too complex, too slow, too many features we didn't need.

We figured there had to be a better way. Build tools that do one thing really well. Make them fast. Make them simple. Charge a fair price.

Six years later, we're still here. Still building. Still focused on making software that works the way you think it should.

No VC money. No exit strategy. Just sustainable products built to last.

How We Work

Start with the problem

We don't build features because they're cool. We build them because they solve real problems for real people.

Simple wins

Complexity is easy. Anyone can add more. The hard part is taking things away until all that's left is what matters.

Speed is a feature

Fast software feels better. It's more respectful of your time. We obsess over performance from day one.

Ship and learn

The best way to find out if something works is to put it in front of people. We ship early, listen hard, and iterate fast.

Sustainable, not hyped

We're not chasing trends or VC funding rounds. We build profitable products that work for the long term.

Your data is yours

We don't sell your data. We don't train AI models on your content. We make money by building good software, not exploiting your trust.

The Team

We're a small team of developers, designers, and product thinkers. We work remotely from different time zones, which means someone's always awake when something breaks (which it rarely does, but still).

We don't do the corporate thing. No pointless meetings. No "syncing up." We communicate asynchronously, work when we're most productive, and ship when it's ready.

We're here to build great products, not to play startup theater.
